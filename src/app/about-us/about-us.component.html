About Us

N.T. R. Arts is an Indian film production company, located at Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. Established by actor Nandamuri Kalyan Ram, it is named after his grandfather Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao.Nandamuri Kalyan Ram established the production house with the name of his grandfather, actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao, known as 'NTR'.
